Education

Elementary School

Sir Samuel B. Steele Jr. P. S.

From junior kindergarten to 6th grade, I attended Sir Samuel B. Steele Jr. P. School, a K-6 school located within walking distance to my house. In grade 4, I enrolled in its Extended French program, where half of the subjects taught in class were spoken in French. I enjoyed the program very much, as it gave me the oppurtunity to explore a new language and culture.



Middle School

Sir Ernest Macmillan Sr. P. S.

For two years, from grade 7 to 8, I attended Sir Ernest Macmillan, a middle school situated right next to my elementary school. This meant that most of my friends from 6th grade would attend Macmillan as well. Despite already being familiar with most of the people in my class, I met several other students from neighbouring elementary schools and became well-acquainted with many students in my grade. I also continued the Extended French program for the entirety of my two years at the school.
