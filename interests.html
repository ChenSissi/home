Interests and Hobbies

Volleyball

I started playing volleyball (not competitively) when I was in 5th grade and since then, it has been one of my favorite sports, along with tennis and ultimate frisbee. I am left-handed, which many people say is advantageous, although my lack of skill probably evens out the advantage. When the weather is nice, I enjoy going out to practice with some friends. There is something magical about hitting a ball with full force towards an opposing team that always brings a smile to my face :).


Reading

When I'm not drowning in homework or watching Youtube videos, you'll most likely find me huddled up under my blankets, like a hermit, immersed in a story. I enjoy books of all genres, from romance, to thriller, to action. However, I find that I tend to enjoy books that are either overly romantic or excessively gruesome and bloody. There is no in-between.

I am not currently reading any books, due to a busy schedule, although I am planning on reading titles such as "The Handmaid's Tale" and "Fahrenheit 451" when I get the chance.


Mangas, Webtoons and Anime

When I previously mentioned that I enjoyed reading, I didn't mention that I mostly read web-comics and mangas. As the case with novels, I mainly read/watch action, thriller or romance genres, although anything is fine as long as there is an interesting plot.

I am currently watching "Banana Fish", an action/crime-thriller anime about mafia organizations situated in New York. Although the main characters get killed like mosquitoes, I am enjoying it so far.
